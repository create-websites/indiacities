Sikkim is a beautiful state in India that is known for its stunning natural beauty. The state is home to some of the most beautiful beaches, lakes, and rivers in the country. Here is a list of the best beaches, lakes, and rivers in Sikkim along with information on when to visit and whether they are good for swimming. Gurudongmar Lake: This is one of the most beautiful lakes in Sikkim and is located at an altitude of 17,800 feet. The lake is surrounded by snow-capped mountains and is considered to be one of the holiest lakes in the region. The best time to visit Gurudongmar Lake is from April to June and September to November. However, due to the extreme cold temperature, swimming is not recommended. Tsomgo Lake: This is another beautiful lake in Sikkim that is located at an altitude of 12,400 feet. The lake is surrounded by mountains and is considered to be a sacred lake by the locals. The best time to visit Tsomgo Lake is from April to June and September to November. However, due to the religious significance and unpredictable weather, swimming is not recommended. Khecheopalri Lake: This is a beautiful lake that is located at an altitude of 5,500 feet. The lake is surrounded by forests and is considered to be a sacred lake by the locals. The best time to visit Khecheopalri Lake is from March to June and September to November. However, due to the religious significance and conservation efforts, swimming is not recommended. Teesta River: This is a beautiful river that flows through Sikkim and is known for its scenic beauty. The river is surrounded by mountains and is a popular spot for rafting and kayaking. The best time to visit Teesta River is from October to December and March to May. Swimming is allowed in designated areas, but caution is advised. Rangit River: This is another beautiful river that flows through Sikkim and is known for its scenic beauty. The river is surrounded by mountains and is a popular spot for rafting and kayaking. The best time to visit Rangit River is from October to December and March to May. Swimming is allowed in designated areas, but caution is advised. Mangan River: This is a beautiful river that flows through Sikkim and is known for its scenic beauty. The river is surrounded by mountains and is a popular spot for rafting and kayaking. The best time to visit Mangan River is from October to December and March to May. Swimming is allowed in designated areas, but caution is advised.
