Til Gud Recipe for Lohri (Sesame & Jaggery Ladoo)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sesame seeds
  • ¾ cup jaggery
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • Optional nuts

Method

  • Roast sesame
  • Melt jaggery with ghee
  • Mix
  • Shape ladoos
  • Cool and store
