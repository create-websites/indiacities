"Natural Wonders of Nagaland: Beaches, Lakes, and Rivers"

Nagaland is a beautiful state in Northeast India with several natural wonders. Here are some of the best beaches, lakes, and rivers in Nagaland:

  • Dzukou Valley: This valley is known for its stunning natural beauty and is a popular trekking destination. The best time to visit is from June to September, but swimming is not recommended.
  • Tsokum Lake: This lake is located in the Phek district of Nagaland and is surrounded by lush green forests. The best time to visit is from April to June, but swimming is not recommended.
  • Shilloi Lake: This lake is considered sacred by the local people and is surrounded by beautiful hills. The best time to visit is from October to February, but swimming is not recommended.
  • Doyang River: This river is a popular spot for fishing and boating, and is also good for swimming. The best time to visit is from October to February.
  • Tizu River: This river is known for its crystal clear waters and is a popular spot for swimming and fishing. The best time to visit is from October to February.
