Gachibowli


IT Hub of Hyderabad

Gachibowli is a well known area in Ranga reddy district of Hyderabad. It is one of the fastest growing  IT hub with MNC’s like Microsoft, Wipro, Infosys,  Capegemini, Polaris, Bank of America ,Franklin Templeton and many more. Gachibowli has developed rapidly in the span of 4­-5 years. Indian School of Business, one of the top ranked business schools in India, has a big campus in Gachibowli. University of Hyderabad is also located here.

Hyderabad has really well built roads. Gachibowli is also infrastructural sound and commuting is not a problem here. The area is very well connected with other parts of Hyderbad in the form of Transportation. There are many options available for commuters like MMTS train, public buses, private cabs, auto rickshaws etc. Gachibowli is also well connected with the International Airport through the newly built highway. The newly built highway provides better connectivity and saves time of commuters.

Gachibowli is also a good and secure area for residential purpose .Because of the big MNC’s, real estate market is growing here rapidly. There are many options available like villas, apartments in gated communities or single family homes in non-gated communities. These villas and apartment nowadays offer world class amenities like Gym, Spa, Swimming pool, Restaurant, parlors, jogging tracks, tennis and basketball courts, 24 hrs Water etc. IVRCL, L&T, Lanco Hills have provided residence to many people here. Other residential projects by reputed builders are also commencing here for those who wants to buy a brand new house.

Gachibowli is a IT hub as well as premier residential location in Hyderabad

Gachibowli is conveniently located near Schools, Shopping malls, Restaurants, Hospitals, Banks, Beauty

Hyderabad is famous for its Biryani. Most of the restaurants here serve the authentic Hyderabadi Biryani.  For others there are plenty of restaurants in the vicinity serving from the South Indian Food to North Indian food, Mughlai, Italian, Burgers, Pizzas, and Chats etc.

 

Apollo Hospital, Care Hospital is some of the best Hospital near Gachibowli. These Hospitals also have emergency rooms facilities.

Hi-Tech city , Madhapur, Jubliee hill, Banjara hill, Mehndipattnam,  Kotaguda, Indiranagar, Kukatpally , Panjagutta  are some of the near surrounding areas of Gachibowli.

Golconda fort, Shilparaman, Durgamchevru, Hussain Sagar, and Ramoji Rao Filmcity are some of the Toursit attractions near Gachibowli.

 

Hyderabad has established itself as the leading destination for IT and IT-enabled services, BPO and entertainment industries. Madhapur is home to the IT industry in Hyderabad. The heart of this area is called HITEC City which has the highest concentration of IT/ITES establishments in the city. An aggressive promotion of growth in this area has led civic boosters to call the city of Hyderabad as "Cyberabad".

Madhapur has transformed from a small rocky village in the early nineties into a modern IT and BPO hub. Madhapur has made its mark on the city map by having the highest concentration of IT/ITES companies in Hyderabad city. It has also become the preferred meeting place in India as well as in Asia with many International conferences and meetings taking place at Hitex Convention Center and Hotel Novotel, both world class establishments situated in this area. This suburb is also home to NIFT (National Institute of Fashion Technology) which is a premier institution for fashion designing and Manthan International School, which is a leading new International Schools in Hyderabad.

Top IT/ITES & Telco companies from all over the world are located in this suburb fuelling the software and BPO businesses. Cyber Towers, Cyber Gateway and Cyber Pearl were the first IT structures to come up. Later on big names like Tata Communications, Accenture, Deloitte, Amazon.com, Convergys, Oracle Corporation, IBM, Dell, Novartis and many others have made the city their business centre. Real estate is on the rise with host of many others constructing skyscrapers, luxury homes, SEZs etc.

Madhapur is well connected with other parts of the city through APSRTC, a state owned bus service. Madhapur is served by MMTS train service; the station is Hi-Tech Station, which is a kilometer away. Taxis and autos shuttle between the station and the city are pretty good. Many new developments are taking place such as metro is being planned to make transportation easier. Inner and outer ring roads are being developed to ease traffic during peak hours, and also to provide connectivity to the new Hyderabad International Airport near Shamshabad.The electrical supplies in the area are unable to keep up with the rapid development and so the utilities have asked IT firms to operate on generators for two hours each day.

Many important landmarks of this suburb are -Lake Durgam, located close to the Hi-tech city, Cyber towers, Shilparamam Auditorium, Arts and crafts center, State Gallery of Arts.
