"Scenic Train Journeys in Meghalaya: Affordable and Breathtaking"


Train Journey Special Features When to Visit Affordability
Guwahati to Shillong Scenic views of hills, waterfalls and valleys October to May Affordable
Shillong to Cherrapunji Views of lush green forests, waterfalls and living root bridges October to May Affordable
Guwahati to Jowai Views of the Umiam Lake and the Jaintia Hills October to May Affordable
Guwahati to Tura Views of the Garo Hills and the Brahmaputra River October to May Affordable

Meghalaya offers some of the most scenic train journeys in India. The Guwahati to Shillong train journey is one of the most popular ones, offering breathtaking views of hills, waterfalls and valleys. The Shillong to Cherrapunji train journey is another must-do, with views of lush green forests, waterfalls and living root bridges. The Guwahati to Jowai train journey offers views of the Umiam Lake and the Jaintia Hills, while the Guwahati to Tura train journey offers views of the Garo Hills and the Brahmaputra River.

The best time to visit Meghalaya for these train journeys is from October to May, when the weather is pleasant and the views are clear. These train journeys are also quite affordable, making them accessible to all kinds of travelers.
