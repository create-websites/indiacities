Scenic Places, Lakes, and Hiking in Tripura

Place Description Best Months to Visit Where to Stay What to Do Important Tips
Neermahal Palace A beautiful palace located in the middle of Rudrasagar Lake October to March Neermahal Tourist Lodge Boating, sightseeing, and photography Carry sunscreen and insect repellent
Dumboor Lake A large lake surrounded by hills and forests October to March Dumboor Lake Tourist Lodge Boating, fishing, and birdwatching Carry warm clothes as it can get chilly at night
Unakoti A historic site with rock-cut sculptures and waterfalls October to March Agartala or Kailashahar Hiking, sightseeing, and photography Carry comfortable shoes and water bottles
Jampui Hills A scenic hill station with panoramic views and orange orchards October to March Jampui Hills Tourist Lodge Hiking, trekking, and camping Carry warm clothes and rain gear as it can rain unexpectedly
Trishna Wildlife Sanctuary A dense forest with diverse flora and fauna October to March Belonia or Udaipur Jungle safari, birdwatching, and nature walks Follow the rules and regulations of the sanctuary
