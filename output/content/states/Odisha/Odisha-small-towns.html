"Affordable Small Towns in Odisha for Retirement and Travel"


Small Towns in Odisha that are Affordable for Visiting and Retirement

Town Speciality Important Tips
Puri Famous for Jagannath Temple and beaches Visit during off-season for cheaper accommodation and avoid crowded areas
Konark Home to the Sun Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site Plan your visit during the annual Konark Dance Festival for a cultural experience
Cuttack Known for its silver filigree work and Durga Puja celebrations Explore the local markets for unique souvenirs and immerse yourself in the festive atmosphere during Durga Puja
Rourkela A steel city with scenic parks and waterfalls nearby Visit the Mandira Dam and Khandadhar Waterfall for a refreshing break from the city
Bargarh Famous for the Dhanu Yatra festival, the world's largest open-air theatre Plan your visit during the festival to witness the reenactment of Lord Krishna's childhood in Mathura
