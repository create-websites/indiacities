Lohri Rituals & Traditional Customs

  • Light the Lohri bonfire after sunset.
  • Offer prasad: til, gur, peanuts, popcorn, rewri and gajak.
  • Parikrama: circle the bonfire 3 or 5 times.
  • Folk songs and community gathering.
  • Dance: Bhangra and Gidda.
