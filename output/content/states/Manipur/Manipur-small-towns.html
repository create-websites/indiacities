Affordable Small Towns in Manipur for Visiting and Retirement


Small Towns in Manipur that are Affordable for Visiting and Retirement

Town Speciality Important Tips
Ukhrul Known for its scenic beauty and cultural heritage. The town is surrounded by hills and is home to the Tangkhul Naga tribe. Visit during the Shirui Lily Festival in May-June to witness the blooming of the rare Shirui Lily. Also, try the local delicacies like smoked meat and bamboo shoot pickle.
Chandel Famous for its natural beauty and historical significance. The town is home to the Kuki tribe and has several waterfalls and caves. Visit the Khangkhui Cave and the Tengnoupal viewpoint for breathtaking views. Also, try the local rice beer and smoked fish.
Moirang Known for its rich history and cultural significance. The town is home to the Loktak Lake, the largest freshwater lake in Northeast India. Visit the INA Memorial Complex to learn about the Indian National Army's role in the freedom struggle. Also, take a boat ride on the Loktak Lake and try the local fish curry.
Thoubal Famous for its handloom and handicraft products. The town is home to the Meitei community and has several temples and markets. Visit the Waithou Lake and the Thoubal Bazar for shopping. Also, try the local sweets like chak-hao kheer and singju.
