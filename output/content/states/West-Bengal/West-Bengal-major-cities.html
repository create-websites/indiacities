Best Cities in West Bengal

City Months to Visit Where to Stay What to Do Important Tips
Kolkata October to March The Oberoi Grand, Taj Bengal, The Park Kolkata Visit Victoria Memorial, St. Paul's Cathedral, Howrah Bridge, Dakshineswar Kali Temple, and enjoy street food at Park Street Be cautious of pickpockets in crowded areas and avoid traveling alone at night
Darjeeling April to June and September to December Mayfair Darjeeling, Windamere Hotel, Cedar Inn Take a ride on the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, visit Tiger Hill for sunrise, explore the tea gardens, and go trekking in the nearby hills Carry warm clothes as the temperature can drop at night, be careful of altitude sickness
Shantiniketan October to March Bolpur Lodge, Mark & Meadows, Camellia Hotel & Resort Visit Visva Bharati University, explore the Tagore Museum, attend the Poush Mela festival, and shop for handicrafts at Kala Bhavan Respect the local customs and traditions, avoid littering in public places
Murshidabad October to March The Rajbari Bawali, The Mango Grove, The Garden Bungalow Visit Hazarduari Palace, Katra Mosque, Nasipur Palace, and explore the silk weaving industry Be respectful of the local culture and dress modestly, carry insect repellent as the area is prone to mosquitoes
