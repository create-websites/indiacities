"Rajasthan's Best Lakes and Rivers: A Review"


Destination Best Time to Visit Swimming Description
Pushkar Lake October to March No Pushkar Lake is a sacred lake in the town of Pushkar. It is surrounded by 52 ghats and hundreds of temples. The lake is considered to be very holy and is a popular pilgrimage site for Hindus. Swimming is not allowed in the lake.
Nakki Lake November to February No Nakki Lake is a beautiful lake located in Mount Abu. It is surrounded by hills and is a popular tourist destination. Boating is allowed in the lake but swimming is not recommended.
Man Sagar Lake October to March No Man Sagar Lake is a man-made lake located in Jaipur. It is famous for the Jal Mahal, a palace located in the middle of the lake. The lake is not suitable for swimming but boating is allowed.
Balsamand Lake October to March No Balsamand Lake is a beautiful lake located in Jodhpur. It was built in the 13th century and is surrounded by lush green gardens. Boating is allowed in the lake but swimming is not recommended.
Gadsisar Lake November to February No Gadsisar Lake is a man-made lake located in Jaisalmer. It was built in the 14th century and is surrounded by temples and shrines. Boating is allowed in the lake but swimming is not recommended.
Chambal River November to February No Chambal River is a beautiful river located in the state of Rajasthan. It is known for its scenic beauty and is a popular destination for bird watching. Swimming is not recommended in the river due to strong currents.
