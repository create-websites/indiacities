Scenic Places, Lakes, and Hiking in Punjab

Place Best Months to Visit Accommodation Options Activities
Amritsar October to March Hotels, Guesthouses, Homestays Visit Golden Temple, Jallianwala Bagh, Wagah Border, and enjoy local food
Dalhousie March to June, September to December Hotels, Resorts, Homestays Hiking, Trekking, Sightseeing, and enjoying the scenic beauty of the Himalayas
Manali March to June, September to December Hotels, Resorts, Homestays Trekking, Hiking, Paragliding, Skiing, and enjoying the scenic beauty of the Himalayas
Chandigarh October to March Hotels, Guesthouses, Homestays Visit Rock Garden, Sukhna Lake, Rose Garden, and enjoy local food
Ludhiana October to March Hotels, Guesthouses, Homestays Visit Nehru Rose Garden, Phillaur Fort, and enjoy local food
Pathankot October to March Hotels, Guesthouses, Homestays Visit Ranjit Sagar Dam, Mukteshwar Temple, and enjoy local food

Important Tips:

  • Carry warm clothes if visiting during winters
  • Carry sunscreen and sunglasses if visiting during summers
  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Carry enough cash as some places may not accept cards
  • Book accommodation in advance during peak season
