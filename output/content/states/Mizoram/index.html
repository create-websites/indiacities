"Discover the Best of Mizoram: Top Places to Visit"


Main Places to See and Visit in Mizoram

Place Description
Aizawl The capital city of Mizoram, Aizawl is known for its beautiful landscapes, vibrant culture, and delicious food. Visitors can explore the city's many markets, museums, and temples, or take a scenic drive to nearby villages and towns.
Phawngpui National Park Located in the southern part of Mizoram, Phawngpui National Park is home to a variety of flora and fauna, including rare orchids, rhododendrons, and the endangered Himalayan black bear. Visitors can go trekking, camping, and birdwatching in the park.
Murlen National Park Spread over an area of 200 sq km, Murlen National Park is known for its dense forests, steep hills, and stunning waterfalls. The park is home to several species of animals, including the clouded leopard, serow, and barking deer.
Palak Lake Located in the Champhai district of Mizoram, Palak Lake is a popular tourist destination known for its scenic beauty and peaceful surroundings. Visitors can go boating, fishing, and camping on the lake's shores.
Tamdil Lake Surrounded by lush green hills, Tamdil Lake is a picturesque spot located in the Aizawl district of Mizoram. Visitors can go boating, fishing, and picnicking on the lake's shores, or explore the nearby forests and waterfalls.
Reiek A small village located near Aizawl, Reiek is known for its stunning views of the surrounding hills and valleys. Visitors can go trekking, camping, and rock climbing in the area, or explore the village's traditional Mizo culture.
