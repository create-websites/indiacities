"Thrilling Theme Parks in West Bengal"


Theme Parks and Amusement Parks to Visit in West Bengal

Park Name Location Months to Visit Popular Rides
Nicco Park Kolkata October to February Water Coaster, Cyclone, Giant Wheel, Flying Saucer
Aquatica Kolkata March to June Black Hole, Wave Pool, Tornado, Niagara Falls
Wonderland Siliguri October to March Roller Coaster, Ferris Wheel, Bumper Cars, Pirate Ship
Millennium Park Durgapur November to February Dragon Coaster, Octopus, Columbus, Break Dance
Science City Kolkata Year-round Space Odyssey, Time Machine, Earthquake Simulator, 3D Vision Theater

West Bengal offers a variety of theme parks and amusement parks for visitors to enjoy. Nicco Park, located in Kolkata, is a popular destination with thrilling rides such as the Water Coaster, Cyclone, Giant Wheel, and Flying Saucer. Aquatica, also located in Kolkata, is a water park with exciting rides like the Black Hole, Wave Pool, Tornado, and Niagara Falls. Wonderland in Siliguri offers a range of rides including the Roller Coaster, Ferris Wheel, Bumper Cars, and Pirate Ship. Millennium Park in Durgapur has rides like the Dragon Coaster, Octopus, Columbus, and Break Dance. Science City in Kolkata is a unique park that combines entertainment with education, featuring attractions like the Space Odyssey, Time Machine, Earthquake Simulator, and 3D Vision Theater. The best time to visit these parks varies, with some parks being open year-round and others having specific months when they are open. Visitors should check the park's website for specific information on hours and admission prices.
