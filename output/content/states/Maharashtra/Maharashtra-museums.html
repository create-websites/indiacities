"Top Historic Sites in Maharashtra"


Museum/Historic Place Location Special Features
Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya Mumbai Artifacts from ancient Indus Valley Civilization, Mughal Empire, and Maratha Empire
Ajanta and Ellora Caves Aurangabad Buddhist, Hindu, and Jain rock-cut temples and monasteries dating back to 2nd century BCE
Shaniwar Wada Pune 18th century palace of the Peshwas with intricate carvings and a light and sound show
Gateway of India Mumbai Iconic arch monument built in 1924 to commemorate the visit of King George V and Queen Mary
Elephanta Caves Mumbai Rock-cut temples dedicated to Lord Shiva dating back to 5th century CE
Raigad Fort Raigad 17th century fort and former capital of the Maratha Empire with stunning views of the Sahyadri Mountains
Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus Mumbai UNESCO World Heritage Site and iconic railway station with Victorian Gothic architecture
