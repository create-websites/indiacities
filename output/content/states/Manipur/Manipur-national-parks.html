"Explore the Best Parks in Manipur: Wildlife, State, and National"


Wildlife Parks, State Parks, and National Parks to Visit in Manipur

Park Name Months to Visit Time Required Important Tips
Keibul Lamjao National Park November to April 1-2 days Carry warm clothes as it can get chilly in the evenings. Hire a local guide for a better experience.
Yangoupokpi-Lokchao Wildlife Sanctuary November to March 1-2 days Carry insect repellent and wear comfortable shoes for trekking. Avoid visiting during monsoon season.
Khonghampat Orchidarium April to June Half a day Visit early in the morning to avoid crowds. Do not pluck or damage the orchids.
Shirui National Park May to July 1-2 days Carry warm clothes as it can get cold at night. Hire a local guide for a better experience.
Khongjom War Memorial Anytime Half a day Wear comfortable shoes as there is a lot of walking involved. Carry water and snacks.
