Mizoram is a beautiful state in Northeast India that is known for its stunning landscapes, rich culture, and warm hospitality. One of the best ways to explore the beauty of Mizoram is by taking a train journey through the state. Here are some of the best train journeys in Mizoram:

  • Silchar to Bairabi: This train journey passes through lush green forests, tea gardens, and beautiful valleys. It is a great way to experience the natural beauty of Mizoram. The best time to visit is from October to March, when the weather is pleasant. This train journey is also quite affordable.
  • Bairabi to Sairang: This train journey offers stunning views of the Tuirial River and the surrounding hills. It is a great way to experience the natural beauty of Mizoram. The best time to visit is from October to March, when the weather is pleasant. This train journey is also quite affordable.
  • Sairang to Bhairabi: This train journey traverses through the picturesque landscapes of Mizoram, including the famous Vantawng Falls. It is a great way to experience the natural beauty of Mizoram. The best time to visit is from October to March, when the weather is pleasant. This train journey is also quite affordable.
