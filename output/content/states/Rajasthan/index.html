Discover Rajasthan's Top Tourist Destinations


Place Description
Jaipur Known as the Pink City, Jaipur is famous for its stunning palaces, forts, and temples. The City Palace, Hawa Mahal, and Amber Fort are must-see attractions.
Udaipur Also known as the City of Lakes, Udaipur is famous for its beautiful lakes, palaces, and gardens. The City Palace, Lake Pichola, and Jag Mandir are popular tourist spots.
Jaisalmer Known as the Golden City, Jaisalmer is famous for its sand dunes, forts, and havelis. The Jaisalmer Fort, Patwon ki Haveli, and Sam Sand Dunes are popular tourist attractions.
Pushkar Known for its holy lake and Brahma Temple, Pushkar is a popular pilgrimage site for Hindus. The Pushkar Lake, Brahma Temple, and Pushkar Camel Fair are must-see attractions.
Ranthambore National Park Famous for its tigers, Ranthambore National Park is a popular wildlife destination in Rajasthan. Visitors can go on safari tours to spot tigers, leopards, and other wildlife.
Ajmer Known for the Ajmer Sharif Dargah, a popular Sufi shrine, Ajmer is a popular pilgrimage site for Muslims. The Dargah Sharif, Ana Sagar Lake, and Taragarh Fort are popular tourist spots.
