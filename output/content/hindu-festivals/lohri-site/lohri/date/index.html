Lohri Date – When Is Lohri Celebrated?

Lohri is celebrated every year on 13 January across India, especially in Punjab and North Indian states. The festival is observed in the evening when families and communities light a bonfire and perform traditional rituals.

Lohri and Makar Sankranti

Lohri is celebrated one day before Makar Sankranti (usually on 14 January). Lohri is mainly an evening folk celebration, while Makar Sankranti is typically a daytime solar festival.
