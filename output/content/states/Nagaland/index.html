Discover Nagaland's Top Attractions


Main Places to See and Visit in Nagaland

Place Description
Kohima The capital city of Nagaland, Kohima is known for its rich history and culture. The Kohima War Cemetery, Naga Heritage Village, and the State Museum are some of the must-visit places in Kohima.
Mokokchung Mokokchung is the cultural center of Nagaland and is known for its vibrant festivals and traditional handicrafts. The Longkhum Village, Mokokchung District Museum, and the Chuchuyimlang Village are some of the popular tourist attractions in Mokokchung.
Tuophema Village Tuophema Village is a traditional Naga village that offers a glimpse into the unique culture and lifestyle of the Naga people. Visitors can stay in traditional Naga huts, participate in cultural activities, and enjoy local cuisine.
Dzukou Valley The Dzukou Valley is a picturesque valley located on the border of Nagaland and Manipur. Known for its stunning natural beauty, the valley is a popular trekking destination and is home to a variety of flora and fauna.
Touphema Tourist Village The Touphema Tourist Village is a unique eco-tourism destination that offers visitors a chance to experience the traditional Naga way of life. Visitors can stay in traditional Naga huts, participate in cultural activities, and enjoy local cuisine.
