Sikkim is a beautiful state in India that is known for its stunning landscapes and scenic beauty. One of the best ways to explore the beauty of Sikkim is by taking a train journey. Here are some of the best train journeys in Sikkim: 1. Toy Train from New Jalpaiguri to Darjeeling: This train journey is one of the most popular train journeys in Sikkim. The train passes through scenic tea gardens, loops and tunnels, offering stunning views of the Himalayas. The best time to visit is from March to June and September to December. The train journey is affordable and is a must-do for anyone visiting Sikkim. 2. Mountain Railways of India from Siliguri to Darjeeling: This train journey is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and passes through picturesque landscapes. The train journey is expensive but is worth every penny. The best time to visit is from March to June and September to December. 3. Train from New Jalpaiguri to Gangtok: This train journey offers stunning views of the Himalayas and Teesta River. The best time to visit is from March to June and September to December. The train journey is affordable and is a great way to explore the beauty of Sikkim. Overall, train journeys in Sikkim are a great way to explore the beauty of the state. Whether you are looking for an affordable train journey or a luxurious one, Sikkim has something to offer for everyone.
