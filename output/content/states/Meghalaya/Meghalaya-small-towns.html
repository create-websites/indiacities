"Affordable Gems: Small Towns in Meghalaya for Visiting and Retirement"


Small Towns in Meghalaya that are Affordable for Visiting and Retirement

Town Speciality Important Tips
Cherrapunji Known as the wettest place on earth, Cherrapunji offers stunning views of waterfalls and lush greenery. It is also home to the living root bridges, a unique natural wonder. Visit during the monsoon season to witness the full beauty of the waterfalls. Wear comfortable shoes for trekking to the root bridges.
Mawlynnong Voted as the cleanest village in Asia, Mawlynnong is a picturesque town with well-maintained gardens and bamboo walkways. It is also close to the natural wonder of the Dawki river. Respect the local culture and customs. Do not litter and maintain the cleanliness of the village.
Tura Tura is a bustling town with a rich cultural heritage. It is home to the Garo tribe and offers a glimpse into their unique way of life. It is also close to the Nokrek National Park, a biodiversity hotspot. Try the local cuisine and interact with the friendly locals to learn more about their culture. Visit the national park for a chance to spot rare wildlife.
Shillong The capital city of Meghalaya, Shillong is a vibrant town with a mix of modern and traditional elements. It is known for its music scene and is often called the "Rock Capital of India". Explore the local markets for unique souvenirs and try the street food. Attend a live music performance to experience the local culture.
