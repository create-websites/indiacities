Kerala is known for its scenic beauty and diverse landscapes. One of the best ways to explore the state is through its train journeys. Here are some of the best train journeys in Kerala:

  • Nilgiri Mountain Railway: This train journey is a must-do for anyone visiting Kerala. The train passes through scenic tea plantations, tunnels, and bridges. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and offers breathtaking views of the Western Ghats. The best time to visit is from October to June. The train is affordable, with tickets starting from INR 415 for a one-way journey.
  • Alappuzha to Kollam: This train journey takes you through the backwaters of Kerala, offering a glimpse of the local life and culture. The train passes through small towns and villages, and you can see the locals going about their daily lives. The best time to visit is from November to February. The train is very affordable, with tickets starting from INR 30 for a one-way journey.
  • Shoranur to Nilambur: This train journey takes you through dense forests, waterfalls, and bridges. It offers a chance to spot wildlife, including elephants and monkeys. The best time to visit is from October to March. The train is very affordable, with tickets starting from INR 30 for a one-way journey.
  • Kannur to Thiruvananthapuram: This train journey takes you through the Western Ghats, offering breathtaking views of the mountains and valleys. The train passes through small towns and villages, and you can see the locals going about their daily lives. The best time to visit is from October to March. The train is affordable, with tickets starting from INR 100 for a one-way journey.
  • Mangalore to Cochin: This train journey takes you through the coastal towns of Kerala, offering a glimpse of the beaches and fishing villages. The train passes through small towns and villages, and you can see the locals going about their daily lives. The best time to visit is from November to February. The train is affordable, with tickets starting from INR 100 for a one-way journey.

Overall, train journeys in Kerala are a great way to explore the state and its diverse landscapes. They are affordable, offer breathtaking views, and give you a chance to experience the local life and culture. So, if you are planning a trip to Kerala, make sure to include a train journey in your itinerary.
