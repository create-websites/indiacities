Discover Uttarakhand's Museums and Historic Sites


Museums and Historic Places to Visit in Uttarakhand

Name Description Location
Government Museum The museum houses a collection of archaeological artifacts, ancient sculptures, and paintings. It also has a section dedicated to the flora and fauna of Uttarakhand. Dehradun
Kumaon Regimental Centre Museum The museum showcases the history and achievements of the Kumaon Regiment of the Indian Army. It has a collection of weapons, uniforms, and photographs. Ranikhet
Robber's Cave Also known as Gucchupani, this cave is a natural formation that is believed to have been used by robbers in the past. It has a stream flowing through it and is a popular picnic spot. Dehradun
Chaukhamba Viewpoint The viewpoint offers a panoramic view of the Chaukhamba mountain range. It is a popular spot for trekking and camping. Chopta
George Everest House The house was built by Sir George Everest, a surveyor who measured the height of Mount Everest. It is now in ruins but offers a great view of the surrounding mountains. Mussoorie
