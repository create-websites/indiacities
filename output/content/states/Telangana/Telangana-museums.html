"Discover Telangana's Rich History: Must-Visit Museums and Monuments"


Museums and Historic Places to Visit in Telangana

Name Description Location
Chowmahalla Palace A palace of the Nizams of Hyderabad, featuring stunning architecture and beautiful gardens. Hyderabad
Charminar A historic monument and mosque, known for its four minarets and intricate carvings. Hyderabad
Salar Jung Museum A museum featuring a vast collection of art, artifacts, and antiques from around the world. Hyderabad
Warangal Fort A historic fort built during the Kakatiya dynasty, featuring impressive architecture and intricate carvings. Warangal
Golconda Fort A historic fort known for its impressive architecture and stunning views of Hyderabad. Hyderabad
Qutb Shahi Tombs A collection of tombs and mausoleums of the Qutb Shahi dynasty, featuring beautiful architecture and intricate carvings. Hyderabad
Kakatiya Rock Garden A unique garden featuring sculptures made from rocks and boulders, inspired by the Kakatiya dynasty. Warangal
