"Discover Madhya Pradesh's Rich History: Must-Visit Museums and Monuments"


Museums and Historic Places to Visit in Madhya Pradesh

Name Location Description
Khajuraho Group of Monuments Chhatarpur District A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Khajuraho Group of Monuments is a collection of Hindu and Jain temples known for their intricate carvings and sculptures.
Gwalior Fort Gwalior A hill fort in Gwalior, the Gwalior Fort is known for its stunning architecture and rich history. It houses several palaces, temples, and monuments.
Madhya Pradesh Tribal Museum Bhopal The Madhya Pradesh Tribal Museum is dedicated to the state's tribal communities and showcases their art, culture, and traditions through exhibits and artifacts.
Sanchi Stupa Sanchi A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Sanchi Stupa is a Buddhist monument known for its ancient architecture and intricate carvings. It is one of the oldest stone structures in India.
Ujjain Ujjain Ujjain is an ancient city in Madhya Pradesh known for its rich history and religious significance. It is home to several temples, including the Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga Temple.
