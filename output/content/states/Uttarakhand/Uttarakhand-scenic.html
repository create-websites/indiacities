"Discover Adventure and Serenity in Uttarakhand"


Scenic Places, Lakes, and Hiking in Uttarakhand

Months to Visit Where to Stay What to Do Important Tips
March to June Jim Corbett National Park, Nainital, Mussoorie, Rishikesh Hiking, wildlife safaris, boating, camping, river rafting, bungee jumping Carry light cotton clothes, sunscreen, and insect repellent
July to September Valley of Flowers, Auli, Chopta, Lansdowne Trekking, sightseeing, photography Carry rain gear and warm clothes as it can get chilly at higher altitudes
October to February Mukteshwar, Kausani, Almora, Bhimtal Sightseeing, skiing, snowboarding, bird watching Carry warm clothes and snow gear if planning to visit higher altitudes

Uttarakhand is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. The state is home to some of the most scenic places, lakes, and hiking trails in India. Whether you are looking for a peaceful retreat or an adrenaline-pumping adventure, Uttarakhand has something for everyone.

Some of the must-visit places in Uttarakhand include Jim Corbett National Park, Nainital, Mussoorie, Rishikesh, Valley of Flowers, Auli, Chopta, Lansdowne, Mukteshwar, Kausani, Almora, and Bhimtal. Each of these places has its own unique charm and attractions.

When it comes to accommodation, Uttarakhand offers a wide range of options to suit every budget and preference. From luxury resorts to budget hotels, homestays, and campsites, you can find it all here.

Some of the popular activities to do in Uttarakhand include hiking, wildlife safaris, boating, camping, river rafting, bungee jumping, trekking, sightseeing, skiing, snowboarding, and bird watching. The state is also known for its rich culture and heritage, and you can explore the local traditions and customs by visiting the various temples, ashrams, and museums.

Before planning your trip to Uttarakhand, it is important to keep in mind the weather conditions and pack accordingly. Carry comfortable clothes, sturdy shoes, sunscreen, insect repellent, and rain gear depending on the season and activities planned. It is also advisable to book your accommodation and activities in advance to avoid any last-minute hassles.
