Lohri Essay – 10 Lines on Lohri Festival

  1. Lohri is a popular harvest festival celebrated in North India.
  2. It is mainly celebrated in Punjab, Haryana, and Himachal Pradesh.
  3. Lohri is observed every year on 13 January.
  4. The festival marks the end of winter and the beginning of longer days.
  5. People light a bonfire on Lohri evening.
  6. Sesame seeds, jaggery, peanuts, and popcorn are offered to the fire.
  7. People sing folk songs and perform traditional dances.
  8. Lohri celebrates gratitude toward nature and the Sun.
  9. It is an important festival for farmers.
  10. Lohri spreads joy, unity, and happiness.
