"Affordable Gems of Kerala: Small Town Travel Guide"


Small Town Speciality Affordability Important Tips
Alappuzha Backwaters, beaches, and houseboats Affordable Visit during the off-season to save money on accommodations and activities.
Fort Kochi Colonial architecture, art galleries, and seafood Affordable Explore the town on foot to fully appreciate its charm and history.
Palakkad Temples, waterfalls, and wildlife sanctuaries Affordable Plan your visit during the monsoon season to see the waterfalls at their best.
Wayanad Tea and coffee plantations, wildlife, and trekking Affordable Book your accommodations in advance, especially during peak season.
Kollam Beaches, backwaters, and historic sites Affordable Take a boat ride on the backwaters to see the town from a different perspective.
