Scenic Places, Lakes, and Hiking in West Bengal

Place Best Months to Visit Where to Stay What to Do Important Tips
Darjeeling March to May and September to November Hotels and homestays Visit Tiger Hill, ride the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, explore tea gardens, and go trekking Carry warm clothes, be prepared for steep slopes and high altitude
Mirik Lake March to June and September to December Resorts and guesthouses Boating, horse riding, and hiking Carry sunscreen and insect repellent, avoid visiting during monsoon season
Sandakphu April to June and September to November Trekking lodges and campsites Trek to the highest peak in West Bengal, enjoy panoramic views of the Himalayas Carry warm clothes, be prepared for high altitude and rugged terrain
Neora Valley National Park October to March Forest lodges and homestays Spot rare birds and animals, go trekking and camping Observe park rules, carry warm clothes and rain gear, avoid visiting during monsoon season
Sunderbans September to March Resorts and eco-tourism lodges Explore the mangrove forests, spot tigers and other wildlife, go boating and birdwatching Observe park rules, carry insect repellent and sunscreen, avoid visiting during monsoon season
