Lohri Speech – Speech on Lohri Festival

Short Speech

Good morning respected teachers and my dear friends. Today, I am going to speak about Lohri, a popular harvest festival celebrated in North India.

Lohri is celebrated every year on 13 January. It marks the end of winter and the beginning of longer days. On Lohri evening, people light a bonfire and offer sesame seeds, jaggery, peanuts, and popcorn. People sing folk songs and perform dances like Bhangra and Gidda.

Thank you and Happy Lohri to everyone.
