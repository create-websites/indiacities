"Discover Tripura's Top Attractions"


Place Description
Ujjayanta Palace A former royal palace, now a museum showcasing the history and culture of Tripura. The palace is known for its beautiful architecture and lush gardens.
Neermahal Palace A stunning palace built in the middle of a lake, Neermahal is a popular tourist destination in Tripura. Visitors can take a boat ride to the palace and explore its beautiful architecture and surroundings.
Unakoti A historic site with rock-cut sculptures and carvings dating back to the 7th-9th centuries. Unakoti is known for its unique and intricate artwork, and is a must-visit for history and art enthusiasts.
Sipahijala Wildlife Sanctuary A wildlife sanctuary home to a variety of animals and birds, including elephants, deer, monkeys, and peacocks. Visitors can take a safari ride through the sanctuary and enjoy the natural beauty of the area.
Chittagong Hills A scenic hill range located in the southern part of Tripura, Chittagong Hills is known for its beautiful landscapes and waterfalls. Visitors can go trekking or hiking in the hills and enjoy the stunning views.
