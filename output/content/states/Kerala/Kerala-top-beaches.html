Best Beaches, Lakes, and Rivers in Kerala

Kerala is known for its beautiful beaches, serene lakes, and winding rivers. Kovalam Beach, located in Thiruvananthapuram, is one of the most popular beaches in Kerala. It is best to visit between September and March when the weather is pleasant and the sea is calm. Varkala Beach, located in Varkala, is another popular beach that is known for its stunning cliffs and clear waters. It is best to visit between October and March.

Alleppey Beach, located in Alleppey, is a beautiful beach that is known for its picturesque sunsets. It is best to visit between November and February. Muzhappilangad Beach, located in Kannur, is the only drive-in beach in Kerala. It is best to visit between October and April.

Pookode Lake, located in Wayanad, is a serene lake that is surrounded by lush greenery. It is a popular spot for boating and picnicking. It is open year-round, but swimming is not allowed. Vembanad Lake, located in Kumarakom, is the largest lake in Kerala. It is best to visit between November and February. Swimming is not allowed in the lake.

Periyar River, located in Idukki, is a winding river that is known for its scenic beauty. It is best to visit between June and September when the river is at its fullest. Swimming is not allowed in the river.
