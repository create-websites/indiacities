"Top 5 Water Destinations in October-March"


Destination Best Time to Visit Swimming
Neermahal Beach October to March Yes
Kalapania Nature Park October to March No
Dumboor Lake October to March Yes
Rudrasagar Lake October to March Yes
Gumti River October to March Yes

Neermahal Beach is a beautiful beach located near the Neermahal Palace. The best time to visit is from October to March. The beach is good for swimming.

Kalapania Nature Park is a scenic park with a lake and waterfall. The best time to visit is from October to March. Swimming is not allowed in the lake.

Dumboor Lake is a large lake with beautiful surroundings. The best time to visit is from October to March. The lake is good for swimming.

Rudrasagar Lake is a serene lake with a palace on an island in the middle. The best time to visit is from October to March. The lake is good for swimming.

Gumti River is a popular spot for picnics and boating. The best time to visit is from October to March. The river is good for swimming.
