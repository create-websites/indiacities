"Top Tamil Nadu Cities: Where to Stay and What to Do"


Best Cities in Tamil Nadu

City Months to Visit Where to Stay What to Do Important Tips
Chennai November to February Hotels in T. Nagar or Egmore Visit Marina Beach, Fort St. George, Kapaleeswarar Temple, and shop at Pondy Bazaar Carry sunscreen and stay hydrated as it can get hot and humid
Madurai October to March Hotels near Meenakshi Temple Visit Meenakshi Temple, Thirumalai Nayakkar Palace, and Gandhi Memorial Museum Dress modestly while visiting temples and carry a hat or umbrella for shade
Coimbatore September to March Hotels in RS Puram or Gandhipuram Visit Marudamalai Temple, Black Thunder Water Park, and Kovai Kutralam Falls Carry insect repellent as the city is known for mosquitoes
Pondicherry October to March Hotels in White Town or Auroville Visit Aurobindo Ashram, Promenade Beach, and French War Memorial Carry a valid ID proof as the city has frequent police checks
Thanjavur November to February Hotels near Brihadeeswarar Temple Visit Brihadeeswarar Temple, Thanjavur Palace, and Saraswathi Mahal Library Carry comfortable shoes as the city has many historical sites to explore
