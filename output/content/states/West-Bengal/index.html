"Discover West Bengal: From Hill Stations to Heritage Cities"


Place Description
Darjeeling Darjeeling is a hill station known for its tea plantations, scenic beauty, and the famous Darjeeling Himalayan Railway. Visitors can take a ride on the toy train and enjoy the stunning views of the Himalayas.
Sunderbans The Sunderbans is a mangrove forest and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It is home to the Royal Bengal Tiger and a variety of other wildlife. Visitors can take a boat ride through the forest and spot the wildlife.
Kolkata Kolkata is the capital city of West Bengal and is known for its rich history, culture, and architecture. Visitors can explore the Victoria Memorial, St. Paul's Cathedral, and the Howrah Bridge. The city is also famous for its street food and sweets.
Shantiniketan Shantiniketan is a small town known for its connection to Rabindranath Tagore, a famous poet and philosopher. Visitors can explore the Tagore Museum, the Visva Bharati University, and the beautiful gardens and sculptures.
Murshidabad Murshidabad is a historical city known for its connection to the Nawabs of Bengal. Visitors can explore the Hazarduari Palace, the Katra Mosque, and the Nizamat Imambara. The city is also famous for its silk and handloom products.
