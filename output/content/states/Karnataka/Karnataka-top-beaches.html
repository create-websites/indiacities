"Top Swimming Destinations in Karnataka"


Destination Best Time to Visit Swimming Description
Gokarna Beach October to March Yes Gokarna Beach is a serene and secluded beach located in the small town of Gokarna. It is known for its clear blue waters and pristine white sand. The beach is perfect for swimming and sunbathing. It is also a popular spot for camping and trekking.
Kudle Beach October to March Yes Kudle Beach is a beautiful and peaceful beach located in Gokarna. It is known for its stunning sunsets and calm waters. The beach is perfect for swimming and surfing. It is also a popular spot for yoga and meditation.
Om Beach October to March Yes Om Beach is a picturesque beach located in Gokarna. It is named after its shape, which resembles the Hindu symbol 'Om'. The beach is perfect for swimming and sunbathing. It is also a popular spot for water sports like surfing and parasailing.
Lalbagh Lake October to March No Lalbagh Lake is a beautiful lake located in the heart of Bangalore. It is known for its lush greenery and serene surroundings. The lake is perfect for a leisurely stroll or a picnic. However, swimming is not allowed in the lake.
Bheemeshwari River October to March Yes Bheemeshwari River is a scenic river located in the Mandya district of Karnataka. It is known for its crystal clear waters and abundant wildlife. The river is perfect for swimming, fishing, and river rafting. It is also a popular spot for camping and trekking.
Cauvery River October to March Yes Cauvery River is a majestic river that flows through the state of Karnataka. It is known for its scenic beauty and rich cultural heritage. The river is perfect for swimming, fishing, and river rafting. It is also a popular spot for camping and trekking.
