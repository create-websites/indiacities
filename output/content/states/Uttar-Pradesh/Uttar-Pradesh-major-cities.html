"Top 5 Must-Visit Cities in Uttar Pradesh"


Best Cities in Uttar Pradesh

City Months to Visit Where to Stay What to Do Important Tips
Agra October to March Hotel Taj Resorts, The Oberoi Amarvilas Visit the Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, Fatehpur Sikri, and enjoy the local street food Book your tickets to the Taj Mahal in advance, carry sunscreen and water, be cautious of touts and pickpockets
Varanasi October to March Hotel Surya, BrijRama Palace Take a boat ride on the Ganges, visit the Kashi Vishwanath Temple, attend the Ganga Aarti ceremony, and explore the narrow lanes of the old city Dress conservatively, be respectful of local customs and traditions, avoid drinking tap water, and beware of scams
Lucknow October to March Taj Mahal Lucknow, Renaissance Lucknow Hotel Visit the Bara Imambara, Chota Imambara, Rumi Darwaza, and enjoy the local cuisine Be cautious of traffic, carry cash as many places do not accept cards, and dress modestly
Allahabad October to March Hotel Kanha Shyam, Hotel Polo Max Take a dip in the Sangam, visit the Allahabad Fort, Anand Bhawan, and explore the Khusro Bagh Carry sunscreen and water, dress modestly, and be respectful of local customs and traditions
Mathura October to March Brijwasi Royal, Hotel Goverdhan Palace Visit the Krishna Janmabhoomi Temple, Dwarkadhish Temple, and explore the local markets Be cautious of touts and pickpockets, dress modestly, and carry cash as many places do not accept cards
