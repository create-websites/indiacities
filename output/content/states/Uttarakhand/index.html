Discover Uttarakhand's Top Destinations


Place Description
Haridwar Haridwar is a holy city situated on the banks of the Ganges river. It is famous for its ghats, temples, and the evening Ganga Aarti.
Rishikesh Rishikesh is a spiritual town known as the Yoga Capital of the World. It is famous for its ashrams, yoga centers, and adventure sports like river rafting and bungee jumping.
Dehradun Dehradun is the capital city of Uttarakhand. It is famous for its scenic beauty, colonial architecture, and educational institutions like the Forest Research Institute.
Mussoorie Mussoorie is a hill station known as the Queen of Hills. It is famous for its scenic beauty, colonial architecture, and tourist attractions like Kempty Falls and Gun Hill.
Nainital Nainital is a hill station known as the Lake District of India. It is famous for its scenic beauty, lakes, and tourist attractions like Naini Lake, Naina Devi Temple, and Tiffin Top.
Jim Corbett National Park Jim Corbett National Park is a wildlife sanctuary known for its tigers, elephants, and other animals. It is famous for its jeep safaris, bird watching, and nature walks.
