Welcome to world's largest integrated film studio Ramoji Film City situated in Hayathnagar and Peddamberpet on Hyderabad. This film city was established by Shri Ramoji Rao Chairman of Ramoji Group in 1996 which was a result of Groups Association with Indian Cinemathrough Usha Kiran Movies. They have produced over 80 films in various languages like Hindi, Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Marathi and Bengali. The area of Ramoji Film City is over 2000 acres of land. It is a recreation centre and a popular tourism spot for complete family of all age groups. Along with amusement park it has both natural and artificial attractions and set ups which will surprise you

It is about 25 kms from Hyderabad which is a historic City and a International IT Hub. Ramoji film city facilitates pre and post productions, they have about 50 studio floors, hi- tech laboratories, outdoor locations, a digital film and the support system so on. The infrastructure includes mock-ups, set construction, film processing unit, custom designed locations cameras etc. As the setup is vast 40 Indian and 20 international films can be produced simultaneously. Over a million tourists visit every year which generates revenues in billions of rupees. At the entrance of the city there are various facilities to visit film city. One can visit by a private car or buy the tourist's coaches. For accommodation there are 3 star and 5 star Hotels providing world class hospitality services. They do have the honeymoon packages and banquet facilities too.

The tour includes many exiting spots such as Hawa Mahal, Golconda fort, Japanese Garden, ETV Planet, Carved Caves, Artificial Waterfalls, Hospital setups Railway stations, an Airport Terminal, US and European sets, temples , mosques, churches shopping complexes etc. There is replica of Vrindavan Garden of Mysore, Mughal Garden of Delhi. The gardens are really very well manicured and hats off to the cleanliness maintained in the city. Inside the city there many restaurants catering one can choose according to their taste. Many activities are organised for the entertainment like cultural shows, magic show, adventure, garden maze, stunts, music attracts people of all age groups. The staffs of the film city are so efficient that they escort you and guide you constantly about all the services and facilities, which is very appreciable. Preferably to visit Ramoji Film city you need one whole day to experience the creativity and the state of art of the city.
