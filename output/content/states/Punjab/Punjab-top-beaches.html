Top 10 Must-Visit Places in Punjab


Destination Best Time to Visit Swimming Description
Goindwal Baoli October to March No Ancient stepwell located in Tarn Taran district. It is a popular pilgrimage site for Sikhs.
Ropar Wetland November to February No A Ramsar site and a popular birdwatching destination. It is home to various migratory birds.
Bhakra Dam October to March No One of the highest gravity dams in the world. It is a popular tourist spot and offers scenic views of the Sutlej river.
Virasat-e-Khalsa October to March No A museum dedicated to the history and culture of Punjab. It is located in Anandpur Sahib and is a must-visit for history buffs.
Amritsar Heritage Walk October to March No A guided tour of the old city of Amritsar. It covers various historical and cultural landmarks of the city.
Anandpur Sahib April to June No A holy city and a popular pilgrimage site for Sikhs. It is home to various historical gurudwaras and temples.
Golden Temple October to March No A holy shrine and a symbol of Sikhism. It is located in Amritsar and is one of the most visited tourist destinations in Punjab.
Pushpa Gujral Science City October to March No A science museum and amusement park located in Kapurthala. It is a popular destination for families with kids.
Wagah Border October to March No A border crossing between India and Pakistan. It is famous for the daily flag-lowering ceremony.
Chandigarh Rose Garden February to April No A botanical garden and a popular picnic spot. It is home to various species of roses and other flowers.
